ABOUT SATWIKSAIRAJ

Satwiksairaj Rankireddy (born 13 August 2000) is an Indian badminton player.
He and his partner, Chirag Shetty, are the only doubles pair from India to become World No. 1 in BWF World Ranking, win BWF World Tour 1000 series, and became the first Indians to win a gold at the Asian games in badminton.
He achieved a new Guinness World Record for the fastest hit in badminton by a male player, with his smash reaching a speed of 565 km/h.


Rankireddy hails from a town named Amalapuram in the state of Andhra Pradesh and started playing badminton after following in the footsteps of his father, who was a state-level player in the past, as well his elder brother.
In 2014, he joined the Pullela Gopichand Academy in Hyderabad and decided to become a doubles specialist
